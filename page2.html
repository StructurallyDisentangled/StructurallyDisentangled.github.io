Segmentation

Segmentation of 3D objects in scenes using our full approach in comaprison to baselines. Our approach is shown in the first row, DFF is shown in the second row, and DFF+RefNeRF is shown in the third row. The first 6 scenes are real world scenes from the real-world RefNeRF dataset and MipNeRF-360 dataset. The remaining scenes are synthetic scenes from the shiny blender dataset. The DFF and DFF+RefNeRF baselines mask the intended objects partially or include parts of other regions. In contrast, our method produces a more accurate and complete segmentation of the desired region.
